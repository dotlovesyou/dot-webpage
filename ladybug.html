Meet Dot

The lucky ladybug who found a unicorn friend.

Who is Dot?

Dot is a cheerful ladybug who lives in the Spotted Gardens, just beyond the edge of Pippin's Wobbly Worlds. She believes that every creature, no matter how small, can make a big difference.

A Special Friendship

One sunny day, Dot met Pippin the Unicorn while exploring a meadow of wildflowers. Despite their differences—one tiny with spots, one majestic with a horn—they became the best of friends.

Dot's Philosophy

"Count your spots, not your troubles." Dot teaches us that even the smallest acts of kindness ripple outward, creating patterns of joy that connect us all.

The Spotted Gardens

Dot's home is a magical place where flowers bloom in polka-dot patterns and the sun always seems to shine just right. Visitors are always welcome to rest their wings.

Thank you for visiting. Every little spot of kindness makes the garden bloom brighter.
